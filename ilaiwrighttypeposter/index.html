A cup of clarendon

THEE slab serif type face, Clarendon is a redesign of a historical typeface so influential that its name was used for all bracketed slab serifs of its era. Even if you haven'e noticed, Clarendon shows up all around you. Have you ever seen the SONY logo? Starbucks logo? Wells Fargo? What about a children's book? I bet you have.

BIG HAPPY FAMILY

There are many fonts within the Clarendon family. Its sisters, brothers, and cousins include: Clarendon URW, Job Clarendon, Clarendon wide, and Clarendon Text, just to name a few. You may even see a couple here.
