Hi! My name is Clarendon.

As THE slab serif typeface, I am a redesign of a seasoned typeface that made such an impact on typography that its name was used for all bracketed slab serifs of its time. Yes, I've had a little work done between the 1830s-40s and the 1930s-50s. How else do you think I still look so young and fresh? That's where I, the IT Girl, come into play. I possess the perfect combination of strength and curves that keep me relevant in the typographic community. Even if you haven't noticed, I show up all around you. Have you ever seen the SONY logo? Starbucks logo? Wells Fargo? What about a children's book? Yep, that's me! Now let me tell you a little bit about myself and where I came from.
